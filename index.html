Retro Tetris Clone

A pure‑bred 1984 Tetris experience, now on the web (HTML5/JavaScript) and Android (Kotlin/Jetpack Compose), served in monochrome glory. Stack your blocks exactly how Alexey Pajitnov intended—no wall‑kicks, no bias, just seven tetrominoes dancing in a 10×20 grid. Hit arrow or tap/swipe on‑screen controls to move and rotate; watch rows vanish and score quietly tick up. Perfect for demoing classic game logic, teaching collision detection, or reliving that block‑drop adrenaline.
